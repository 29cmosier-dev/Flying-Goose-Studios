How do I post a moment?

If you want to post a moment, navigate to the moments page, then, on the right of the screen you should see an area where you can submit your own moment. After you click submit, our team will take some time to reveiw it, and will either approve it or delete it. This process may take up to 24 hours.

Why are my chats not appearing in my chats list?

The reason why you are not seeing your chats could be because:

The chat was deleted

Occasionaly, our admins/moderators may delete chats to ensure community saftey.

You are not friends with the user you are trying to chat with

Another reason you may not see your chat is beacause you are not friends with the user you are trying to chat with. Please become friends with that user first, by navigating to the friends page and either sharing your friend code with your friend, or asking your friend for their friend code.
